## Potential Enhancements
- **PDF export**: Generate printable layouts and export full books as PDF.
- **Audio narration**: Text-to-speech narration with per-page playback controls.
- **User accounts**: Authenticated profiles with per-family libraries and sharing controls.
- **More themes**: Holidays, seasons, friendship, school day, bedtime routines, pets.
- **Additional art styles**: Paper cutout, claymation, crayon, soft 3D.
- **Multiple children**: Support siblings or friends as recurring characters and sidekicks.
- **Retry controls**: Regenerate individual pages or images without re-running the full pipeline.
- **Offline cache**: Prefetch/save assets for bedtime use without network hiccups.
